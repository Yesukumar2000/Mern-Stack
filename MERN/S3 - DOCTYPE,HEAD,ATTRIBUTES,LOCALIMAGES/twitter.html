Twitter App

Twitter, founded in 2006 by Jack Dorsey, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams, has established itself as a leading platform for real-time communication, news dissemination, and public discourse. With its characteristic brevity and focus on short-form content, Twitter enables users to share thoughts, opinions, and updates with a global audience.In October 2022, billionaire Elon Musk acquired Twitter

In India, Twitter has gained widespread popularity as a platform for staying informed, engaging in conversations, and expressing viewpoints on a wide range of topics, including politics, entertainment, sports, and culture. Its user base includes individuals, public figures, organizations, and media outlets, contributing to a diverse and dynamic ecosystem.
